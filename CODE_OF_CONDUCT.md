# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

GenixBit Labs Private Limited and the maintainers of this project are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We expect participation that contributes to an open, inclusive, collaborative, and healthy community.

## Expected behaviour

Examples of behaviour that supports this community include:

- Showing empathy, patience, and respect toward other people
- Giving and receiving constructive technical feedback professionally
- Focusing disagreements on ideas, evidence, and project outcomes
- Respecting different viewpoints, experiences, languages, and skill levels
- Accepting responsibility, apologising when appropriate, and learning from mistakes
- Protecting confidential information, credentials, personal data, and security reports
- Using examples and claims that are accurate, lawful, and not misleading

## Unacceptable behaviour

Unacceptable behaviour includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, discrimination, or sustained disruption
- Sexualised language, imagery, or unwanted sexual attention
- Insults, personal attacks, trolling, or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Publishing another person's private information without explicit permission
- Sharing credentials, customer information, confidential code, or private communications
- Submitting malicious scripts, hidden payloads, deceptive instructions, or prompt-injection content
- Impersonation, plagiarism, fabricated proof, fake customer claims, or manipulated contribution history
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, commits, release channels, project events, and other spaces where someone represents the project or GenixBit community.

## Reporting

Report abusive, threatening, discriminatory, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour privately to **info@genixbit.com** with the subject:

`Code of Conduct report: GenixBit/agency-agents`

Please include relevant links, dates, screenshots, and context. Do not publish sensitive reports in a public issue.

All reports will be reviewed as promptly and fairly as practical. The maintainers will protect the privacy and safety of reporters to the extent reasonably possible.

Security vulnerabilities should be reported according to [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Depending on severity and history, consequences may include a private warning, temporary participation restrictions, removal of content, or a permanent ban from project spaces.

Enforcement decisions will consider context, impact, safety, and the need to protect the community and project.

## Attribution

This policy is informed by widely used open-source community standards, including the Contributor Covenant, and has been adapted for the security, privacy, and responsible-AI needs of this project.
